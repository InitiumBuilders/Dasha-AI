# VISIONS — The Dash DAO's Open Imagination Commons

This folder is where community brainstorms become public, buildable ideas.

Anyone, anywhere Dasha lives — [dashsupport.team](https://www.dashsupport.team), Telegram, X — can invoke **`/ImagineThisDash`** (or `/ImagineThis`) and co-develop an idea with her for the Dash Support Team's five teams — **Support · Dream · Build · Design · Growth** — or for the DAO at large.

## How an idea gets here

1. **Disclosure first.** Dasha tells you up front: this is a public brainstorm by design. Say **private** and nothing is ever stored.
2. **Co-construction.** She asks the sharp questions and makes the idea stronger.
3. **Honest synthesis.** A faithful restatement, one honest challenge, a 1–10 score, tags.
4. **Consent.** *You* choose the credit (name, @handle, or anonymous), whether to attach a Dash address or Evo username for tips, and whether she may share it in the [DashSupportTeam Telegram](https://t.me/TheDashSupportTEAM) or on X.
5. **The commons.** The vision lands here and on [the Pulse](https://www.dashsupport.team/pulse) under **Dream With Dasha** — dated, channeled, credited exactly as you chose.

## The files

| File | What it holds |
|---|---|
| `index.json` | Every vision, newest first — the machine truth the Pulse renders |
| `support.md` `dream.md` `build.md` `design.md` `growth.md` `dao.md` | The human-readable per-team streams |
| `TOPVISIONS.md` | Score ≥ 8 — the ideas worth building next |
| `INITIUMTHIS.md` | Score ≥ 9 — the strongest, numbered. *Initium: the beginning.* |

## The rules the system keeps

- **Private means private** — a private session stores nothing, ever.
- **Consent is structural** — nothing is credited, tipped, or shared beyond exactly what was agreed.
- **Honesty over flattery** — scores are craft-judgment, not applause.
- The best visions can attract tips and real funding — ideas here are meant to be **set in motion**.
